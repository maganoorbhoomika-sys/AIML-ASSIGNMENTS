Assignment Name :AI Around Me
Description : List 10 real-life AI applications you use daily and explain how ML might work behind each in 1–2 lines


1. Google Maps
   Google Maps uses machine learning to analyze traffic data, road conditions, and user locations to suggest the fastest route and estimate travel time.

2. YouTube
   YouTube uses ML algorithms to study your watch history, likes, and viewing time to recommend videos that match your interests.

3. Gmail
   Gmail uses machine learning to detect spam emails by learning patterns from millions of emails and filtering unwanted messages automatically.

4. Instagram
   Instagram uses ML to analyze user activities like likes, comments, and shares to show posts that are most relevant to the user.

5. Amazon
   Amazon uses ML-based recommendation systems to suggest products based on your browsing history, previous purchases, and similar user behavior.

6. Spotify
   Spotify uses machine learning to study listening habits and music preferences to create personalized playlists and recommend new songs.

7. Google Photos
   Google Photos uses deep learning and image recognition to identify faces, objects, and locations in photos to organize them automatically.

8. Netflix
   Netflix uses ML algorithms to analyze what users watch and rate, then recommends movies and TV shows that they might like.

9. Google Assistant
   Google Assistant uses speech recognition and natural language processing to understand voice commands and provide appropriate responses.

10. Gboard (Google Keyboard)
    Gboard uses machine learning to predict the next word while typing by learning from typing patterns and commonly used words.
